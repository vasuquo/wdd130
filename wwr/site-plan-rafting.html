Purpose

Usually, sprint and slalom events are conducted for rafting. Sprints are conducted either in the individual format or head-to-head format. In the Head-to-head format, the riders have to move through the easiest route. In the slalom race, the course consists of typical turns and riders have to navigate around them.

Audience

Considerations for the target audience of this site are as follows : Who are the target customers? The target audience are business class people, family, college students and visitors looking for activities while on vacation. Also included are seasoned water rafters. What do they want? They want to be away from their hectic routine work schedule and be with their families. Academic institutions offer outings for their students to improve their mental as well as physical health. People who are looking to challenge themselves on the next big run. What are their needs that aren't being met? Provide good adventure and thrill-seeking activities Provide quality time to bond with friends and family. Provide a fun way for exercising through the physical activity paddling, sunshine and swimming.
